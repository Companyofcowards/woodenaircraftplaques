Our Story

Three Decades of Authentic Filipino Craftsmanship

Our workshop traces its roots to the Clark Air Base ecosystem in Pampanga — a heritage of precision craftsmanship that has been producing commemorative pieces for military squadrons, airlines, and collectors for over three decades.

Every piece that leaves our workshop is supervised by a founder who has spent a career learning to read the grain of Philippine mahogany — one of the finest hardwoods in the world for carving and long-term durability.

We do not use molds, resin, or machine carving. Every silhouette, every detail, every painted livery is done by hand, by people who have done it for years. That is why our pieces feel different when you hold them.
